December 2, 1997

*** Corrections on error criteria ***

Real numbers were identified with zero when they were less than 1.d-40 or sometimes 1.d-50 in small and common groups. This may cause troubles in some compilers. I have set the criterion to 1.d-13. I thank Dr.Tota Nakamura for pointing this out.

October 1, 1992

Troubles were reported in the normalization loops in subroutines. An overflow may happen in loops like

--------------------------------------------------------*

dnorm=0.d0

do 10 j=1,idim

10 dnorm=dnorm+x(j)**2

--------------------------------------------------------*

if some of x(j) are too large, which may be the case in extreme cases. This type of do loop appears in many places. If you encounter an overflow (or underflow) trouble, please try inserting a checking loop before the above loop. For instance,

*--------------------------------------------------------*

absmax=0.d0

do 5 j=1,idim

5 absmax=max(absmax,abs(x(j)))

if(absmax.gt.1.d50)then

do 6 j=1,idim

6 x(j)=x(j)/absmax

end if

*---------------------------------------------------------*

No change will be implemented in the original source code because the above trouble seems a very rare case.
